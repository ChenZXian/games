# Garden Siege

Garden Siege is a lane defense game on a 5x9 grid.

## Controls
- Tap a card to arm plant placement.
- Tap a tile to place a plant if sun and cooldown allow.
- Tap shovel then tap tile to remove a plant.
- Tap sun orbs to collect sun.

## Rules
- Enemies enter from the right and move left.
- If an enemy reaches the base, one heart is lost.
- Lose all hearts to fail.
- Clear all waves to win and unlock next level.

## Plants
- Sunbud creates sun.
- PodShooter fires seed projectiles.
- ThornBlock reflects bite damage.
- FrostMint shoots slowing projectiles.
- BurstBloom explodes after short fuse.

## Progression
- Six levels with increasing wave intensity.
- Best clear time is stored per level.
